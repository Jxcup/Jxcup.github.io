A bionic climbing robot suitable for a cable-stayed bridge

start at 2017-02-01

(Mar. 2017 - Sep. 2017)
Supervised by Dr. Yangjie Cao
We build a hardware and software system, the design of the robot inspired by the inchworm. we construct a two-legged multi-joint robot with three gaits(twist, reverse and inchworm gait). And there is a cloud platform to receive the collected data in real time.
I participate in robot assembly and control, the communication between the upper computer and the cloud platform.
We designed two robots and applied for China’s invention patents, pantent nos: 201710388616.4 and 201810138547.6.

The design details of the mechanical claw part are shown in the following figure. The method shown in the figure adopts some new plastic materials, which can increase the friction of the underlying surface.
